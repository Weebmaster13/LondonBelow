# Journal System

The Journal is the player's soul.

## Canon Meaning

The Journal is not a menu. It is a living record of identity, memory, fear, and loss.

## Expected Behavior

Future Journal systems may show recovered memories, corrupted entries, `???` unknowns, identity percentage, family traces, and replay changes.

## Design Rules

- Unknown entries display as `???`.
- Journal changes should mean something.
- Journal state may affect dialogue, endings, and Director decisions.
- The Journal should grow with the player.

## What It Does Not Own

The Journal does not own gameplay truth by itself. Server systems own truth; presentation systems display it.

## Open Design Questions

- Does the Journal write back to the player?
- What does a nearly empty Journal look like?

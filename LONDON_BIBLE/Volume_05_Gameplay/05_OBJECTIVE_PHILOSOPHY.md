# Objective Philosophy

Objectives should express desire, fear, and emotional stakes.

## Purpose

An objective is not just a task list. It should tell the player what they think they need, while the world reveals what the objective really costs.

## Rules

- Objectives should be clear enough to play.
- Objective wording may change when memory or identity changes.
- Objectives should coordinate with Journal and Director systems.
- Multiplayer objectives must remain server-authoritative.

## Examples Of Direction

- Find a family memory.
- Open a door that should not exist.
- Return something to a room that remembers it.

## Open Design Questions

- Should objective text ever lie?
- Can the Building write objectives?

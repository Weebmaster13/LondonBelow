# Puzzle Philosophy

Every puzzle must reveal story, identity, or emotional truth.

## Purpose

Puzzles are not locks with decoration. They are conversations with the Building, the family, the Journal, or the player's own memory.

## Rules

- Puzzle rooms must protect comprehension and cooperation.
- Solutions should feel thematically connected to the space.
- Failure may create pressure, but not unfair confusion.
- Puzzle state must be server-authoritative.

## Replay

Replay puzzles may reveal earlier clues differently or expose hidden meanings, but should not invalidate first-playthrough clarity.

## Open Design Questions

- Which puzzle types best fit a six-year-old perspective?
- How can puzzles feel emotional without becoming obtuse?

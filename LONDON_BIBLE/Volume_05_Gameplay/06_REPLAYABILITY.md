# Replayability

The first playthrough tells the story. The second playthrough changes the meaning of the story.

## Purpose

Replayability should be emotional and interpretive, not just collectible completion.

## Replay Tools

- Changed Journal entries.
- Newly meaningful memories.
- Different dialogue implications.
- Alternate environmental readings.
- Ending context.
- Director decisions influenced by known history.

## Rules

Replay content must not make the first playthrough feel fake or wasted. It should deepen meaning.

## Open Design Questions

- What does the game remember between playthroughs?
- Which secrets should only appear after an ending?

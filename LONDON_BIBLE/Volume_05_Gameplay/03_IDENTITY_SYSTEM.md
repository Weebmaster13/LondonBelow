# Identity System

Identity percentage shows how much of the player remains.

## Purpose

Identity is a story and systems signal. It should influence Journal state, memory availability, dialogue, endings, and future Director decisions.

## Rules

- Identity is not a generic health bar.
- Identity loss should have causes and meaning.
- Unknown states should be expressed through `???` when appropriate.
- Restoration, if possible, must feel costly or emotionally significant.

## Multiplayer

Future multiplayer identity rules must decide whether identity is individual, party-shared, or both. Server truth must remain authoritative.

## Open Design Questions

- Can a party preserve one player's identity at another player's cost?
- Does low identity make the Building more persuasive?

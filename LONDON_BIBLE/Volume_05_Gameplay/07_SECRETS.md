# Secrets

Secrets should feel like the Building almost failed to hide something.

## Purpose

Secrets can reveal hidden truths, alternate interpretations, family details, Building history, or replay meanings.

## Rules

- Secrets must not be random rewards.
- Secrets should be fair enough to discover with attention.
- Secrets may change Journal state, Identity percentage, or ending context.
- Secrets must preserve canon.

## Secret Categories

- Memory secrets.
- Family secrets.
- Building history secrets.
- Replay-only secrets.
- Environmental meaning secrets.

## Open Design Questions

- Which secret should be found by listening instead of looking?

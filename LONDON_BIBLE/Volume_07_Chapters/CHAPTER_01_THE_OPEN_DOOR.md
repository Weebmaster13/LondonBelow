# Chapter 01: The Open Door

This chapter title is a canon-facing outline, not an implementation plan.

## Emotional Purpose

The first major threshold should teach players that a door can be invitation, warning, trap, and memory at once.

## Creative Ingredients

- The first clear break from home safety.
- A door that should not be open.
- A sense that the Building has accepted the player.
- Early Journal uncertainty.
- Puzzles that reveal why entry matters.

## Boundaries

Do not build Chapter 1 yet. Do not add map geometry, monster AI, final scares, final UI, or Workspace mutation from this outline.

## Open Design Questions

- Who opened the door?
- Was the door waiting for the child specifically?

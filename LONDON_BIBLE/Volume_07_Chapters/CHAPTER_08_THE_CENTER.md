# Chapter 08: The Center

## Emotional Purpose

The Center should feel like reaching the place where the Building's appetite is clearest.

## Creative Ingredients

- Central architecture that feels ancient and impossible.
- Identity and memory pressure near maximum.
- Major truths framed by the Building's perspective.
- Director-controlled restraint before major revelation.

## Boundaries

Do not finalize the Center's physical form yet. It must grow from earlier chapter meanings.

## Open Design Questions

- What sits at the Center?
- Does the main entity fear, serve, or misunderstand the Center?

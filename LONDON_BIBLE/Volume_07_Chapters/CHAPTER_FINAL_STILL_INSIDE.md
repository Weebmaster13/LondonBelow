# Chapter Final: Still Inside

## Emotional Purpose

The final chapter should make players question escape, identity, and the meaning of remembering.

## Creative Ingredients

- Endings shaped by identity, memories, Journal state, and emotional connection.
- Family truth without simple comfort.
- Building perspective versus human truth.
- Replay implications.

## Title Meaning

`Still Inside` should remain open: inside the Building, inside memory, inside grief, inside the Journal, or inside the story the Building tells.

## Open Design Questions

- Can the player truly leave?
- What does Marmalade mean at the end?
- Is remembering always mercy?

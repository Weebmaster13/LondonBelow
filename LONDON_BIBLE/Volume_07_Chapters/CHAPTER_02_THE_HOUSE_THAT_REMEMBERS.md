# Chapter 02: The House That Remembers

## Emotional Purpose

This chapter should make players realize the Building remembers in ways people do not.

## Creative Ingredients

- Domestic spaces that feel familiar but wrong.
- Memory fragments that change room meaning.
- Journal entries with missing or altered words.
- Puzzles about what is remembered versus what is true.

## Building Role

The Building may preserve emotional shape while destroying factual certainty.

## Open Design Questions

- Which family memory returns here?
- What does the house remember incorrectly on purpose?

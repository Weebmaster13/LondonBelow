# Chapter 03: The Streets Below

## Emotional Purpose

The player should feel that London continues beneath itself, older and less human than the city above.

## Creative Ingredients

- Foggy streets that should not exist underground.
- Rain or muffled weather in impossible places.
- Distant lamps and routes that rearrange meaning.
- Cooperation pressure for multiplayer parties.

## World Role

This chapter can expand the scale from home and house into district-level fear.

## Open Design Questions

- Are these streets copied from London or remembered by the Building?
- What sounds should make players think someone is nearby?

# Chapter 00: Home

Chapter 00 is the emotional opening, not a tutorial checklist.

## Purpose

The player spends 10 to 15 minutes in a beautiful London apartment with Mum, Dad, Sister, and Marmalade. The goal is to love the family before horror begins.

## Required Feel

- Warm, safe, lived-in.
- Child-scale interactions.
- Family presence that feels specific.
- Marmalade as comfort.
- Subtle wrongness only after warmth is established.

## Systems Later

Opening actions may feed Journal, Memory, Identity, Observation, and Director systems, but this file does not implement them.

## Open Design Questions

- What exact action ends the safe opening?
- What object from home should echo through later chapters?

# Chapter 04: The Architect's Rooms

## Emotional Purpose

This chapter should make architecture feel intentional, accusing, and designed around the player.

## Creative Ingredients

- Rooms that explain the Building's logic without exposition.
- Blueprints, measurement, impossible proportions, and child-scale distortion.
- Puzzles about construction, omission, and ownership.

## Canon Boundary

The identity of the Architect is not final. Do not invent final lore without explicit approval.

## Open Design Questions

- Is the Architect a person, role, memory, or lie?
- Did anyone build the Building, or only find it?

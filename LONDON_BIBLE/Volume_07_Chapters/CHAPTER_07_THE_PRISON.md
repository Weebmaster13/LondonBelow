# Chapter 07: The Prison

## Emotional Purpose

This chapter should ask what it means to be trapped when the thing holding you is also inside memory.

## Creative Ingredients

- Confinement without generic jail design.
- Identity loss as imprisonment.
- Doors, locks, and keys with emotional meaning.
- Puzzles about permission, guilt, or release.

## Boundaries

Do not make the prison a copied map trope. Its design must belong to London Below's Building logic.

## Open Design Questions

- Who is imprisoned: the player, the family, the entity, or memory itself?
- Can a player free something they should not?

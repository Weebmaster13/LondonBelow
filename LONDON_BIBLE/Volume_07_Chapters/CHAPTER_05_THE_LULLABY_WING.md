# Chapter 05: The Lullaby Wing

## Emotional Purpose

This chapter should turn comfort into unease without making family warmth feel cheap.

## Creative Ingredients

- Soft sounds in unsafe places.
- Familiar rhythm, voice, or nursery-like structure.
- Memory fragments tied to care, sleep, and protection.
- Audio pressure that must remain meaningful.

## Accessibility Rule

Audio-based horror must preserve accessibility options and puzzle comprehension.

## Open Design Questions

- Who sang the lullaby?
- Does the Building understand comfort or only imitate it?

# Chapter 06: Sister's Promise

## Emotional Purpose

This chapter centers Sister as an emotional anchor.

## Creative Ingredients

- Promise, trust, betrayal, or protection.
- A memory the player may not fully understand.
- Journal changes tied to sibling connection.
- Choices that affect identity or ending context.

## Boundary

Sister's exact age, fate, and full role are open. Do not finalize them without explicit story direction.

## Open Design Questions

- What promise was made?
- Was it kept, broken, forgotten, or rewritten?

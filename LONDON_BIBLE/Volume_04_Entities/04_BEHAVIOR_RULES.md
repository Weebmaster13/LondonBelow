# Entity Behavior Rules

## Server Authority

Future entity behavior must be server-authoritative and subordinate to the Director ecosystem.

## Meaning

Entity behavior should communicate intent. Watching, smiling, leaving, returning, chasing, refusing to chase, and alerting must all have design meaning.

## Fairness

Players can be scared without being cheated. Chases need readability. Hiding rules need consistency. Puzzle rooms need protection from unfair pressure.

## Director Coordination

Monster AI must not own horror pacing. Monster Director and Horror Director decide when threat pressure is appropriate. Execution systems perform approved actions.

## Open Design Questions

- What behaviors are reserved for high identity loss?
- How does the entity behave differently toward solo players and groups?

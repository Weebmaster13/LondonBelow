# Main Entity

The main entity is the primary intelligent threat, but it is not the creator of the Building.

## Desired Feel

The entity should toy with players. It can watch, smile, fake leave, return, stalk, learn hiding spots, and sometimes choose not to chase.

## Horror Role

It is frightening because it appears to understand timing, vulnerability, and attention. It should not behave like a simple line-of-sight chase machine.

## Limits

The entity must not own all horror pacing. Horror Director, Monster Director, Environment Director, Lighting Director, Audio Director, and World Intelligence must coordinate pressure.

## Originality Rule

Do not copy entity design, names, behavior, symbols, voices, or presentation from existing games.

## Open Design Questions

- What is the entity's original name, if any?
- Does it remember being human, or is that a trick?
- Why does it use this Building?

# Voices And Mimicry

Voices are dangerous because family warmth is sacred.

## Purpose

Mimicry should make players question safety, memory, and emotional trust. It must not become a random soundboard.

## Rules

- A familiar voice in a wrong place must mean something.
- Repeated voice tricks should evolve or become suspicious.
- Voices should respect puzzle comprehension and accessibility.
- The Journal may react to voice-driven memory pressure.

## Family Voices

Mum, Dad, Sister, and Marmalade-related audio ideas must be handled carefully. The family should not become cheap scare bait.

## Open Design Questions

- Can Marmalade be mimicked?
- Which voice should the player trust least on replay?

# Entity Types

This file defines future entity categories without implementing them.

## Main Entity

The primary threat that stalks, watches, manipulates, and sometimes withholds pursuit.

## Crawlers

Smaller creatures that can scout, alert, mislead, or increase pressure. Crawlers should support the main entity rather than become random attack mobs.

## Voices

Mimicked or displaced voices that may use family warmth against the player. Voice behavior must be meaningful and should not become cheap imitation spam.

## Environmental Presences

Some threats may feel like rooms, shadows, doors, or sounds rather than bodies.

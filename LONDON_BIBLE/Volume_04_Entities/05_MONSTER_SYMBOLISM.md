# Monster Symbolism

Monsters should embody emotional or thematic pressure.

## Symbolic Sources

Future monsters may connect to:

- Forgotten family memory.
- Childhood fear.
- Identity loss.
- The Building's appetite.
- False safety.
- Voices that should be comforting.

## Design Rule

Symbolism should guide behavior, sound, lighting, and encounter design. It should not be decorative lore pasted onto a generic enemy.

## Boundaries

Do not finalize monster models or names here. Do not copy another game's symbols or creature silhouette.

## Open Design Questions

- What does the main entity symbolize most strongly?
- What should crawlers symbolize that the main entity does not?

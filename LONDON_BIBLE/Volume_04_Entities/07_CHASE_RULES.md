# Chase Rules

Chases must be earned, readable, and meaningful.

## Purpose

A chase should be a punctuation mark, not the whole sentence. It should follow pressure, observation, narrative beat, or player choice.

## Fairness

- The route must be readable.
- The player must understand enough to react.
- Multiplayer groups need synchronization rules.
- Safe rooms and puzzle rooms require protection.
- Failure should not feel arbitrary.

## Director Control

Future Monster Director should request and approve chase pressure through the Director ecosystem. Monster AI executes movement and attacks only after approval.

## Open Design Questions

- What is the first real chase?
- Can a chase end because the entity chooses to stop?

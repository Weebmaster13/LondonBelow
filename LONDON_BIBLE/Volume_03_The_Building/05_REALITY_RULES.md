# Reality Rules

London Below should make players question what is real because the world has rules they do not understand yet.

## Core Rule

Reality may bend, but design intent must remain coherent.

## Allowed Distortions

- Rooms that connect impossibly.
- Familiar voices in wrong locations.
- Journal text changing after memory loss.
- Sounds that lead toward emotional truth.
- Lighting that supports pressure but respects safe and puzzle protections.
- Architecture that reflects identity erosion.

## Forbidden Distortions

- Random nonsense with no meaning.
- Cheap confusion used to hide weak design.
- Unfair puzzle corruption that blocks comprehension.
- Scares that contradict established canon without explanation.

## Open Design Questions

- Which reality rules are visible to players?
- Can players learn to predict the Building's behavior?

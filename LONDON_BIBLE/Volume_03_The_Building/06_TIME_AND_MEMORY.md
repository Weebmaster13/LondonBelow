# Time And Memory

Time in the Building should feel connected to memory.

## Memory Rules

Memories are fragments of identity. They are not coins, points, or generic collectibles. Recovering, losing, or corrupting memories should affect Journal state, Identity percentage, dialogue, endings, and future Director decisions.

## Time Rules

The Building may preserve moments, repeat emotional patterns, or make rooms feel older than they should. It should not use time distortion as a random gimmick.

## Replay

Replay can reveal that time behaved differently than the player first believed.

## Open Design Questions

- Are memories stored by the Building or stolen into it?
- Can a memory be recovered without cost?
- Does Marmalade remember differently than people do?

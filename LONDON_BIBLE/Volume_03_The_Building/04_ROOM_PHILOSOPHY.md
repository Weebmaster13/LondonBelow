# Room Philosophy

Rooms in London Below are emotional arguments.

## Purpose

A room should not exist only to hold a key. It should express memory, pressure, family, identity, Building logic, or player vulnerability.

## Room Types

- Safe rooms: temporary recovery, never absolute safety unless explicitly protected.
- Puzzle rooms: comprehension and cooperation must be protected from unfair pressure.
- Memory rooms: spaces that reveal or damage identity.
- Chase routes: spaces designed for pressure, visibility, and escape readability.
- Threshold rooms: spaces that change what the player believes is real.

## Design Rules

- Every important room should have a reason.
- Room affordances are permissions, not automatic actions.
- A room's emotional meaning should guide lighting, audio, puzzle, and monster permissions.

## Open Design Questions

- What makes a room feel like the player's home has been misremembered?
- Which rooms should be replay-sensitive?

# Identity Erosion

Identity erosion is the central damage model of London Below's story.

## Canon Meaning

Identity percentage shows how much of the player remains. It is not a normal health bar.

## What Can Erode

- Name-like self-recognition, even though the player has no canon name.
- Family connection.
- Memory details.
- Journal words.
- Dialogue certainty.
- Ending possibility.

## Design Rules

Erosion should be meaningful and readable over time. It should not feel like arbitrary punishment.

Unknown entries must display as `???`.

## Future Systems

Future save, replay, Journal, Director, and ending systems may consume identity state. They must not treat it as a disposable score.

## Open Design Questions

- Can identity be restored fully?
- What happens at zero percent?

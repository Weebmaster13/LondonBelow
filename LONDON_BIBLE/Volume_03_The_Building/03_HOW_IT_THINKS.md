# How It Thinks

The Building thinks like a place that remembers hunger.

## Perspective

The Building tells truth from its own perspective. It may consider identity something removable, memory something edible, and family connection a structure it can rearrange.

## Intelligence Rules

- It recognizes emotional weight.
- It notices repeated behavior.
- It values memories that define the player.
- It can use architecture as language.
- It can make rooms feel like accusations.

## Not Random

The Building does not create random hallucinations. Its distortions should point toward memory, identity, family, guilt, fear, or hidden truth.

## Future Systems

Observation Engine, World Intelligence, Environment Director, Lighting Director, Audio Director, and future Monster Director may all express aspects of Building intelligence, but no single system owns the entire Building.

## Open Design Questions

- Does the Building understand love or only consume it?
- Can it be wounded, tricked, or satisfied?

# Observation Engine Integration

The Observation Engine turns trusted gameplay facts into knowledge.

## Creative Use

Future systems should emit observations for meaningful player behavior: memory recovery, Journal changes, family-object interaction, lantern overuse, solitude, hesitation, puzzle avoidance, and identity pressure.

## Canon Protection

Observations should describe facts, not invent lore. A system may observe `Memory.FragmentRecovered`; it must not decide final story truth outside canon.

## Examples

- Player reads a family note.
- Player ignores a memory fragment.
- Player stays alone too long.
- Player returns to a room after Journal corruption.

# Save And Replay Future

Save and replay systems must preserve meaning, not just progress.

## Creative Use

Future save state may track memories, Journal changes, Identity percentage, endings, replay unlocks, family connection state, and Director-relevant history.

## Replay Rule

The first playthrough tells the story. The second playthrough changes the meaning of the story.

## Canon Protection

Save data must not reduce memories to currency. Replay data must not break first-playthrough emotional clarity.

## Open Design Questions

- Which truths persist between playthroughs?
- Can the Building remember the player across saves?
- Which ending states unlock replay-only Journal changes?

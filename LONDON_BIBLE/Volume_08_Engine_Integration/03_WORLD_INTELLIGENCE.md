# World Intelligence Integration

World Intelligence gives systems spatial meaning.

## Creative Use

Future world profiles should tell Directors whether a space is home, street, threshold, safe room, puzzle room, chase route, memory room, exterior, interior, or unknown.

## Canon Protection

Affordances are permissions, not actions. A room allowing whispers does not mean whispers must occur. A room allowing monster presence does not mean a monster appears.

## Unknown Spaces

Unknown zones should stay conservative. They should deny major hostile pressure, blackouts, unfair puzzle disruption, and monster-support pressure by default.

# Gameplay Intelligence Integration

Gameplay Intelligence owns reusable truth for objects, doors, inventory, keys, objectives, puzzles, and memory-adjacent gameplay state.

## Creative Use

Future gameplay systems should express Bible meaning through data-driven interactions, not one-off scripts.

## Canon Protection

Door, key, objective, and puzzle systems should not invent story meaning by themselves. They expose trusted facts and request Director approval when a moment affects pacing or horror.

## Examples

- A key represents permission, not just access.
- A door can be a threshold memory.
- An objective can change wording after identity loss.
- A puzzle should reveal emotional truth.

## Open Design Questions

- Which gameplay facts should directly influence Identity percentage?
- How should Journal entries reference objective state?

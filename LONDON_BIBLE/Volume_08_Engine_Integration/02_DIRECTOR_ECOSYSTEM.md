# Director Ecosystem Integration

Directors protect pacing and meaning.

## Creative Use

Future Directors should coordinate horror, environment, lighting, audio, monster pressure, narrative beats, and puzzle protection so the game feels intentional rather than noisy.

## Canon Rule

A Director may approve a scare, sound, lighting change, or monster reveal only if it respects Bible canon, World Intelligence policy, and the Engine Constitution.

## Required Restraint

Major horror moments should be approved, timed, and traceable. Random jumpscare spam violates London Below's identity.

# Canon Status

This file separates locked canon from open design space.

## Locked Canon

- London Below is an original Roblox psychological horror game.
- The player is six years old.
- The player has no canon name.
- The family lives in a beautiful London apartment.
- The family includes Mum, Dad, Sister, and Marmalade, an orange cat.
- The opening lasts 10 to 15 minutes.
- The opening must make the player love the family before horror begins.
- The Journal is the player's soul, not a normal menu.
- Memories are collectible fragments of identity, not coins.
- Unknown entries display as `???`.
- Identity percentage represents how much of the player remains.
- The Building is alive, ancient, and older than the main entity.
- The main entity did not create the Building; it uses it.
- The Building consumes identity, memories, and emotional connections.
- Horror should leave players empty, broken, sad, confused, scared, and wanting to replay.
- Jump scares must be rare, earned, and meaningful.

## Soft Canon

Soft canon is directionally true but may be refined by explicit future design work.

- Victorian London, fog, old architecture, rain, and gaslit atmosphere are central.
- The Building may rearrange memory, space, sound, and emotional truth.
- The family should feel real before they become terrifyingly fragile.
- The main entity should toy with players rather than behave like a simple chase machine.

## Open Design Questions

- The exact family surname.
- The exact historical origin of the Building.
- The final identity of the main entity.
- The exact number and unlock order of endings.
- The rules for how memory fragments alter second-playthrough content.

## Canon Change Rule

Do not change locked canon casually. A canon change requires explicit user instruction and should update every affected Bible file in the same commit.

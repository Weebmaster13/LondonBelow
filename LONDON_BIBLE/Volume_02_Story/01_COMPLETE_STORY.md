# Complete Story

This file is the story container for London Below. It does not finalize every plot answer yet; it defines the canon spine and records open questions.

## Canon Spine

A six-year-old child lives with Mum, Dad, Sister, and Marmalade in a beautiful London apartment. The opening should make the player love the family before horror begins.

The child is drawn into or accepted by a living ancient Building beneath or behind London. The Building consumes identity, memories, and emotional connections. The main entity did not create it; the entity uses it.

The story is about remaining yourself when a place, a monster, and perhaps your own memory are trying to decide who you are.

## Story Principles

- The first playthrough tells the story.
- The second playthrough changes the meaning of the story.
- Memories are not coins; they are identity fragments.
- The Journal is the player's soul.
- Unknown entries display as `???`.

## Not Yet Final

The exact complete plot is intentionally not locked in this foundation pass. Future story work must fill this file without contradicting locked canon.

## Open Design Questions

- What event first connects the family to the Building?
- What does the main entity want from this specific child?
- Which ending, if any, is emotionally true?

# Opening Sequence

The opening sequence lasts 10 to 15 minutes.

## Goal

Make the player love the family before horror begins.

The opening is not filler. It is the emotional foundation for everything the Building later threatens.

## Required Feel

- Beautiful London apartment.
- Warm family presence.
- Ordinary child-scale interactions.
- Marmalade as a real comfort, not a decoration.
- Small details that can later become meaningful.

## Horror Boundary

The opening may contain subtle wrongness, but it should not rush into overt horror. The first fear should feel like something precious has shifted by one inch.

## Future Systems

Opening interactions may later feed Journal state, Memory fragments, Identity percentage, and Director decisions. They should be designed as meaningful observations, not throwaway chores.

## Open Design Questions

- What is the first interactive task in the apartment?
- What is the first sign that the Building is near?
- Does Marmalade react before any human notices?

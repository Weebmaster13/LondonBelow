# Master Timeline

The timeline tracks real events, remembered events, Building-altered events, and replay revelations.

## Timeline Layers

### Family Life

The family lives in a beautiful London apartment. The opening takes 10 to 15 minutes and presents warmth before horror.

### Building History

The Building is ancient and existed before the main entity. Its full historical origin is open.

### Entity Use

The main entity uses the Building. It did not create the Building.

### Player Journey

The player enters a structure that removes identity, memory, and emotional certainty.

### Replay Revelation

On replay, earlier scenes may gain new meaning through changed Journal entries, remembered fragments, alternate dialogue, or newly visible patterns.

## Timeline Rules

- Mark uncertain events as uncertain.
- Do not treat Building perspective as objective truth unless explicitly confirmed.
- Track whether a memory is recovered, distorted, missing, or consumed.

## Open Design Questions

- What is the oldest confirmed date connected to the Building?
- Does the family know anything about the Building before the opening?

# Hidden Truths

Hidden truths are revelations that reframe play without making the first playthrough feel meaningless.

## Purpose

The player should finish the game, think they understand, then replay and realize earlier moments had second meanings.

## Truth Types

- Family memories that were incomplete.
- Journal entries that hid missing words.
- Rooms that represented emotional events.
- Sounds that belonged to specific memories.
- Building statements that were true only from its perspective.

## Rules

Hidden truths must be foreshadowed. They should not feel like random twists.

The Building can distort truth, but the design must track what is objectively true, what is remembered, and what is Building-framed.

## Open Design Questions

- Which hidden truth should be visible in the apartment on replay?
- Which truth should never be fully confirmed?

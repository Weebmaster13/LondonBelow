# The Family

The family is the emotional center of London Below.

## Locked Family Members

- Mum
- Dad
- Sister
- Marmalade, the orange cat

## Purpose

The opening should make the player love the family. The family must feel specific enough to hurt when memory, safety, or trust begins to fail.

## Family Tone

The apartment should feel beautiful, lived-in, and safe. Family warmth should be communicated through ordinary behavior: voices, routines, small jokes, care, objects, and Marmalade's presence.

## Horror Use

Future horror may mimic, distort, withhold, or reframe family memory, but it must not reduce the family to cheap bait. Family horror must reveal emotional truth.

## Open Design Questions

- What is Sister's age and personality?
- What object best represents Mum, Dad, Sister, and Marmalade?
- Which family memory should the player be most afraid to lose?

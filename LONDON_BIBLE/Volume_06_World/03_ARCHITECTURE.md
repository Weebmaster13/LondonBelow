# World Architecture

Architecture should make London Below feel beautiful, old, and wrong.

## Materials And Forms

Future art direction may use brick, iron, wood, glass, wallpaper, stairwells, narrow corridors, heavy doors, gas lamps, wet stone, carriage forms, and impossible extensions.

## Meaning

Architecture should communicate emotional state. A door can feel like refusal. A hallway can feel like memory stretching. A staircase can feel like being led.

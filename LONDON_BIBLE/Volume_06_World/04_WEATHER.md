# Weather

Weather is emotional pressure.

## Core Weather

Fog, rain, damp air, muffled streets, distant thunder, and gaslit haze are central to the London Below mood.

## Design Rules

- Weather should support pacing, not become constant noise.
- Fog can conceal, reveal, isolate, or soften distance.
- Rain can comfort, muffle, or trap.
- Weather should respect accessibility and readability.

## Future Systems

Lighting Director, Audio Director, Environment Director, and World Intelligence may use weather context for approvals, but no weather effect should bypass Director rules.

## Open Design Questions

- Does weather come from London, the Building, or both?
- Can weather remember the family?

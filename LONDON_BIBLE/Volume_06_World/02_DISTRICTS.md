# Districts

Districts are future world-scale context groups for atmosphere, story, and Director policy.

## Purpose

Districts help future systems understand where a player is, what sounds make sense, what lighting is allowed, whether monsters can appear, and what emotional pressure fits.

## Possible District Roles

- Home district.
- Fog street district.
- Building threshold district.
- Underground district.
- Memory district.

## Rules

District definitions must remain original and data-driven through World Intelligence. Districts are permissions and context, not automatic actions.

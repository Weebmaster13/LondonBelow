# Symbolism

Symbols should support meaning without becoming heavy-handed.

## Potential Symbol Domains

- Doors and thresholds.
- Journals and missing words.
- Cats, warmth, and home.
- Fog and uncertain memory.
- Rain and muffled grief.
- Mirrors and identity erosion.
- Keys and permission.

## Rules

Symbols should be repeated with purpose. A symbol that appears everywhere without development becomes noise.

## Open Design Questions

- Which symbol belongs most strongly to Marmalade?
- Which symbol means the Building is lying by omission?

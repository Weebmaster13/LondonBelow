# London

London Below is rooted in London atmosphere without copying real places as exact gameplay spaces unless future design explicitly approves it.

## Tone

Victorian influence, fog, rain, old streets, lamps, brick, iron, wood, glass, narrow passages, and grand interiors should support the mood.

## Relationship To The Building

London may feel like a surface above something older. The Building can sit beneath, behind, inside, or between familiar structures.

## Originality

Do not copy locations from other games. Real London inspiration should be transformed into original London Below spaces.

## Open Design Questions

- Is the apartment in a specific district?
- How visible is ordinary London after the Building takes interest?

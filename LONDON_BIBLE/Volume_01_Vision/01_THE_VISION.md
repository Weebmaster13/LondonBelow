# The Vision

London Below is an original first-person psychological horror experience about a child, a family, and a Building that eats the parts of a person that make them human.

The game should feel beautiful before it feels terrifying. The player begins in warmth: family, home, Marmalade, ordinary London life, and the safety of being loved. Horror begins when that safety becomes uncertain.

## Promise

The player is not only trying to survive. They are trying to remain themselves.

London Below should make players feel empty, broken, sad, confused, scared, and compelled to replay because the second playthrough reveals that earlier moments meant something different.

## What This Is Not

- Not a monster chase clone.
- Not random jumpscare compilation.
- Not a generic haunted house.
- Not a puzzle hallway with horror decoration.
- Not a copy of any Roblox horror game.

## Creative Direction

The strongest version of London Below combines Victorian London atmosphere, family intimacy, identity erosion, meaningful puzzles, and Directors that pace horror with restraint.

## Open Design Questions

- How much of London outside the Building should remain real?
- What visual motif best represents identity loss without feeling generic?

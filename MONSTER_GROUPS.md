# Monster Group Intelligence

Group Intelligence prepares future monster cooperation without networking, NPCs, or execution.

## Owns

- Shared believed facts.
- Investigation claims.
- Duplicate target prevention.
- Claim timeout and cleanup.
- Competition resolution by priority.

## Does Not Own

- Multiplayer networking.
- Monster movement.
- Physical territory blockers.
- NPC coordination animations.
- Client presentation.

## Claim Rules

Claims are bounded and expire. Duplicate claims reject so future monsters do not pile onto the same target without arbitration.

## Future Use

Crawler creatures may eventually alert the main entity by sharing facts. That future system must still route through Directors and preserve server authority.

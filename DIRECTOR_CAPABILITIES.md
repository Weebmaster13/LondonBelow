# Director Capabilities

Capabilities describe what a Director can interpret or approve.

Examples:

- Lighting: brightness, flicker, shadows, emergency lights.
- Audio: whispers, footsteps, heartbeat, fake sounds.
- Music: music state, silence, stingers.
- Monster: reveal, stalk, retreat, attack, fake path.
- Narrative: story beat, dialogue, lore trigger, checkpoint.
- Performance: budgets, throttles, cleanup pressure.

Capabilities are not final behavior. They are contract metadata that lets the Coordinator and future systems route requests safely.

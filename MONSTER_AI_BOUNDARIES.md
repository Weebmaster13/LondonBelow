# Monster AI Boundaries

Monster AI Execution Foundation is subordinate infrastructure.

## Owns

- Server-owned monster execution registrations.
- Approved intent intake.
- Dry-run execution records.
- Future behavior executor interfaces.
- Diagnostics, snapshots, bounded state, validation failures, and self-checks.
- Observation emission for future monster state record changes.

## Does Not Own

- Intent decisions.
- Horror pacing.
- Story reveals.
- Monster Director approval.
- Horror Director approval.
- Living Cognition beliefs.
- Monster Intelligence reasoning.
- Horror Orchestration pressure budgets.
- Navigation or pathfinding execution.
- Movement, attacks, damage, animations, final models, NPC spawning, Workspace mutation, Lighting, Audio, UI, client remotes, final jumpscares, or Chapter content.

## Authority Rules

The server owns all Monster AI execution state. Clients cannot create, approve, mutate, or execute Monster AI intent. No client remotes exist in Phase 17.

## Approval Rules

Every execution request must include `approvedBy` and `approvalId`. Missing approvals reject. Approved intent is still validated and may reject if it is expired, unsafe, duplicated, unsupported, or targets an unknown monster.

## Originality Rule

This foundation supports London Below's original psychological horror identity. It must not copy maps, monsters, names, story, attacks, or scare beats from any other Roblox horror game.

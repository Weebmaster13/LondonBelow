# Identity Runtime

`IdentityRuntime` owns a bounded identity percentage per profile.

Identity percentage is a foundation number for future meaning systems. It is not story canon, not UI, not a fear value, and not Monster AI behavior.

## Rules

- Server owns identity truth.
- Identity is clamped from 0 to 100.
- Increase and decrease operations remain bounded.
- Clients may not own or set identity truth.

## Future Work

Future narrative and replay systems may interpret identity percentage. Those interpretations must stay subordinate to Governance, the London Bible, and server-authoritative save state.

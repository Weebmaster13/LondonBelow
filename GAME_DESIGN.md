# London Below Game Design

London Below is a multiplayer psychological horror game set in a supernatural Victorian London.

## Pillars

- Psychological horror first.
- Multiplayer tension without chaos.
- Exploration, objectives, keys, doors, puzzles, checkpoints, and escape.
- A terrifying main building supported by foggy streets and oppressive atmosphere.
- Systems that feel authored, reactive, and fair.

## Core Loop

1. Players gather in the lobby.
2. Players form a party and enter a chapter.
3. The chapter starts in foggy Victorian streets or a threshold space.
4. Players explore and discover objectives.
5. Keys, doors, puzzles, and environmental changes open deeper paths.
6. The Horror Director escalates and releases tension.
7. Crawlers scout, alert, mislead, or pressure players.
8. The main monster stalks and toys with the party.
9. Players reach checkpoints and attempt escape.
10. The chapter ends in escape, failure, or story-specific resolution.

## Interaction Design

Interactions must be readable, server-validated, and consistent.

- The client can show prompts.
- The server decides whether interaction succeeds.
- Objectives and doors must have clear feedback.
- Puzzles should use clues, environment, sound, notes, and cooperation rather than pure guessing.

## Chapter Design

Chapters should be built as production slices:

- Clear entry and exit.
- Strong map identity.
- Original story fragments.
- Checkpoints that reduce frustration without removing dread.
- Objective pacing that gives the Horror Director room to breathe.
- Multiplayer routes and regrouping moments.

## Originality Rules

Do not copy existing Roblox horror maps, monsters, names, assets, stories, puzzle beats, or chase structure. Inspiration may come from polish and emotional target only.

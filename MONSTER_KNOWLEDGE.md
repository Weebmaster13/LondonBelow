# Monster Knowledge

Knowledge is what a monster believes.

## Knowledge States

- `Known`: high-confidence believed fact.
- `Suspected`: plausible but not certain.
- `Lost`: once-known fact no longer reliable.
- `False`: believed or identified as wrong.
- `Shared`: received from another monster.
- `Unknown`: explicitly unknown.

## Difference From Memory

Memory records experiences. Knowledge summarizes believed facts. This lets monsters be wrong, share uncertain information, and lose confidence over time.

## Safety

Knowledge is bounded per monster. Invalid states, invalid confidence, and missing facts reject.

## Future Use

Future Monster Director and Monster AI can consume knowledge as reasoning context, not as permission to execute actions.

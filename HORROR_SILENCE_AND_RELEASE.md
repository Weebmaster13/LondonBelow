# Horror Silence And Release

Silence and release are first-class horror decisions.

## Silence

Silence can mean do nothing, delay action, suppress a scare, hold pressure, let the player breathe, wait for better timing, or use absence after a major beat.

Silence protects the London Bible rule that horror is not random noise.

## Release

Release intentionally lowers pressure after a chase, puzzle completion, major scare, emotional reveal, long tension period, player overwhelm, party confusion, or repeated failure.

## Why It Matters

Constant pressure makes players numb. London Below should make fear breathe: warmth, unease, pressure, silence, release, and meaning.

# Cognition Certification

Phase 16 includes deterministic self-checks.

## Covered Scenarios

- Contradictory evidence foundation.
- Thought decay.
- Thought merge.
- Thought split.
- Belief reinforcement.
- Belief contradiction.
- Duplicate registration rejection.
- Snapshot isolation.
- Serialization integrity.
- Shutdown cleanup.
- Validation failures.
- Execution leakage rejection.

## Certification Boundary

Certification proves the substrate behaves correctly. It does not certify Monster AI, gameplay, presentation, Chapter content, or final horror behavior.
